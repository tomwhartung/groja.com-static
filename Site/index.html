GRoJAs are Graphical Representations of Jungian Archetypes.

Inspired by the work of Piet Mondrian, Carl Jung, Wassily Kandinsky, Isabel Myers-Briggs, and David Keirsey, the Groja Program © creates Spiritual Portraits of individuals based on their score on the MBTI ® or a similar personality questionnaire.

If you are just curious, please explore the site. If you are ready to see your personality, or that of a friend or loved one, learn how you can get your own personal Groja!

Welcome to the Spiritual Portrait Site

Can we talk? I have always felt like I was on the wrong planet, — do you by any chance know what I mean? Even my parents ... ok let's not go there right now.... Either you know what I'm talking about, or you don't.

I have my own ideas and perceptions, my own questionnaire (not curently online), and ultimately this is my own thing.

All of this is based on Carl Jung's theories so naturally similarities abound. My wish is to honor, carry on, and build on the work of my predecessors without stepping on any toes, legal or otherwise.

When I first learned that I was an INFP, it was life-altering. I would like to help you on a similar journey of greater self-awareness!

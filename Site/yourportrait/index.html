Handmade Portraits

Due to a large variety of reasons, the biggest one being I want total control over the quality of these images, I have decided to create all Spiritual Portraits by hand.

Although a large part of this process is automated by the Groja Program ©, producing high-quality, unique and personalized portraits, and ensuring you understand its significance and meaning requires a personal touch.

Finally, the program contains many options, such as different resolutions and orientations (portrait vs. landscape) not currently demonstrated in the galleries. I can help you decide what sort of image pleases you and ensure it is within your price range before starting on it!
